Welcome to Sapphire Salon

At Sapphire Salon, we pride ourselves on being a nail salon that is owned and operated by women. As an all-woman team, we understand the importance of having an environment that is safe and welcoming for all people. We are dedicated to providing our clients with a space where they feel comfortable and cared for.

Meet Our Team

We have an amazing team of nail technicians that each bring a unique set of skills to the table and can offer your hands the looks you've been dreaming about.

Haylee

Co-founder

Haylee has been providing nails services for 7 years, but has loved doing nail art as a hobby since she was a little girl. If you're looking for a quiet and calm experience, Haylee is your girl!

Marcia

Co-founder

Marcia has been working in the field for 8 years. She specializes in natural, sustainable materials, loves to chat and make you feel welcome! If you're looking for an eco-friendly solution for your nails, Marcia is the person for you.

Karolina

Nail Specialist

Karolina has been working in the field of nail care for 4 years now, and she specializes in French manicures. She is always smiling, always accommodating, and would love to make your next appointment one you won't soon forget!

Gabriela

Nail Specialist

Gabriela has been doing nails for 3 years and specializes in 3D nail art. If you have a design idea or want to try out something new, she's your gal. You'll love her infectious laugh and her willingness to chat with customers--she'll make your experience her at Sapphire Salon unforgettable.
